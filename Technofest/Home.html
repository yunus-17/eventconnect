About the Department

Information Technology (IT) is a branch of engineering that focuses on the development, management, and security of information systems and digital infrastructure. IT engineers work on cutting-edge technologies such as blockchain, IoT, big data, and automation to enhance efficiency, security, and connectivity across industries. As the demand for scalable, secure, and intelligent solutions increases, Information Technology plays a crucial role in shaping the digital future and improving technological advancements worldwide.

About the IT Association

The IT Association serves as a bridge between students and the IT industry by organizing technical seminars, workshops, symposium, industrial visits, guest lectures and cultural activities. It empowers students to develop professional skills, leadership qualities, and stay current with emerging technologies.

About the IT Coding Club

The IT Coding Club nurtures the problem-solving mindset of students by conducting regular coding contests, hackathons, and innovation challenges. It encourages collaboration, hands-on practice, and creative application of coding skills to real-world problems.
